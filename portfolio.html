Critical Habitat Analysis

Add link to project?

In this project, I researched and compiled historic data and present knowledge to create a critical habitat analysis for the eastern spotted skunk. The eastern spotted skunk has been experiencing population-wide decline since the mid-1900s, but they have yet to receive a comprehensive analysis of their current threats and an action plan to promote their survival. My intent was to begin that analysis by identifying their critical habitat in Iowa, ranking habitat suitability across the state, and creating a map in ArcGIS Pro of my findings.
