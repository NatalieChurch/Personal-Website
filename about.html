Video games

Edit text later: Commonly play Hollow knight, animal crossing, stardew valley. I love almost all genres, and am excited to talk about them! The reason video games have such a spot in my heart is because they can be these amazing interactive works of art that inspire and educate in such a fun way! Some games that do this well include Roki (Help a girl rescue her brother while exploring a world rich with Scandinavian folklore!), Gris (Lead a young woman on a journey to reclaim her lost memories and find peace after loss), When the Past Was Around (Help a woman come to terms with loss and reclaim her passion for music), and Gibbon: Beyond the Trees (Wrestle with the reality of environmental degradation as you control a gibbon navigating your once peaceful habitat and the ruins it became).
